OTHER UNIVERSITIES

Here are some other universities that match the topic present here:

In Romania

  1. Universitatea Tehnică din Cluj-Napoca – Facultatea de Arhitectură și Urbanism – main architecture school in Cluj-Napoca with a 6-year integrated program focused on design, theory and urban planning. Offers Erasmus exchanges across Europe.
  2. Ion Mincu University of Architecture and Urban Planning (București) – Romania’s most well-known architecture school, public and specialized entirely on architecture and urbanism. Long tradition and many graduates working in Romanian practice

In Europe

  1. TU Wien – Vienna University of Technology (Austria) – well-regarded technical and design education with strong theory + engineering balance.
  2. Politecnico di Milano (Italy) – design-driven architecture program with lots of international students and strong links to industry.
  3. Delft University of Technology (Netherlands) – highly international, focused on innovation and sustainability in architecture.
  4. Technical University of Berlin (Germany) – rigorous technical and urban design training with good research facilities

In Spain

  1. Universitat Internacional de Catalunya (UIC Barcelona) – private architecture school in Barcelona with practical, international-oriented program, lots of project work and small class groups.
  2. Escuela Técnica Superior de Arquitectura – Universidad de Granada – part of the public Universidad de Granada, offers official architecture studies in a historic city environment combining theory and design.
  3. Escuela Técnica Superior de Arquitectura de Madrid – Universidad Politécnica de Madrid – one of Spain’s oldest architecture schools with large faculty and classical curriculum, strong in urbanism and technical aspects.
