HOW TO GET IN

This is a step by step explantion on how to get in:

  • 1️⃣ Finish High School (Mandatory) You must: Graduate from high school Pass your Bacalaureate (if you’re in Romania) or equivalent diploma Your final exam grade usually counts as part of your admission score.
  • 2️⃣ The Drawing Admission Exam (The Most Important Part) This is the key step. Architecture admission is heavily based on a drawing exam, not just math grades. You’ll usually have: A drawing test (often 1–2 sessions) Tasks like still life drawing or perspective drawing They evaluate: Proportions Perspective Accuracy Line control Light and shadow Spatial understanding This is not about “artistic style.” It’s technical drawing ability. Most students who get accepted: Prepare for 1–2 years Take private architecture drawing classes Practice weekly, sometimes daily If you start preparing at 16, that’s actually a very good time.
  • 3️⃣ Your Admission Average Your final admission score is usually calculated from: Drawing exam grade (big percentage) Bacalaureate grade (smaller percentage) The drawing exam normally weighs more. Because it’s competitive, your score must be higher than other candidates applying that year.
  • 4️⃣ Online Application Process During summer (usually July): You: Create an account on the university admission platform Upload documents (ID, diploma, transcripts, etc.) Pay the application fee Choose Architecture as your specialization After results are published: If you’re admitted, you confirm your place You submit original documents
